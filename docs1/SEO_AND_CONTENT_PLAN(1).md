# SEO_AND_CONTENT_PLAN.md

## SEO objective

Build organic visibility for Galaxy Elite Private Match without turning the platform into a normal property listing portal.

The SEO message should focus on:

```text
Private property matching
Verified property interest
No public listings
Buyer/tenant/investor demand
Landlord availability matching
Transparent licensed agents
Galaxy Elite reviewed matches
```

## Market Pulse content strategy

Market Pulse should publish market intelligence, not private property adverts.

Topics:

- UAE buyer demand trends
- Dubai rental demand by move-in month
- Abu Dhabi villa interest trends
- UK land buyer demand
- Investor demand for off-plan vs secondary
- Commercial office demand signals
- Landlord upcoming vacancy trends
- Agent transparency and verification
- How private property matching works
- Why owners may not want public listings

## Content rules

Allowed:

- Anonymised demand trends
- Price/rent ranges
- Volume trends
- Market commentary
- Education about verification
- Approved public interest cards

Not allowed:

- Private property documents
- Owner names
- Exact private addresses
- Unapproved listings
- Sensitive transaction details
- Misleading availability claims

## Technical SEO requirements

- Use canonical URLs
- Noindex admin/dashboard/match-room/private document routes
- Generate sitemap for public pages only
- Use structured data for Organization, Website, Article, FAQ
- Optimise OpenGraph preview
- Add country landing pages
- Add hreflang later if multilingual/country-specific pages expand
- Ensure performance/Core Web Vitals are good
