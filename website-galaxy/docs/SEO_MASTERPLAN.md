# SEO Masterplan

## Primary positioning

**Private property matching without public listings.**

## Main keywords

- private property matching
- property matching platform
- post property interest
- private property match UAE
- private property match UK
- buyer interest board
- tenant landlord matching
- landlord vacancy matching
- transparent real estate agents
- private property availability

## Page strategy

- `/` — global concept and brand positioning.
- `/post-interest` — demand capture and conversion.
- `/interest-board` — indexable public demand signals.
- `/private-availability` — owners/landlords/developers conversion.
- `/for-agents` — transparent agent policy.
- `/uae`, `/uk`, `/india` — country intent pages.
- `/market-pulse` — newsletter and content engine.

## Content engine

Publish weekly Market Pulse articles:

- UAE buyer demand ranges.
- Dubai tenant move-in demand.
- UK land buyer interest.
- Private landlord vacancy signals.
- Agent transparency guidance.
- Why private property owners do not want public listings.

Do not publish private property details unless the appropriate permissions and permits are in place.
