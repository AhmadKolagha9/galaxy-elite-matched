## Usage rules

- Use gold/ivory logo variants on dark backgrounds.
- Use dark text variants only on cream/pearl backgrounds.
- Keep at least one logo-mark width of clear space around the logo.
- Do not stretch, recolor manually, rotate or add new effects.
- Keep the logo large enough to preserve the building/G details.
